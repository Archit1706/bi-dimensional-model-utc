A single one-way ride on Pace Suburban Bus costs $2.00 for full fare, with reduced fares available for seniors, students, and riders with disabilities.
Full Fare
The standard one-way fare on Pace is $2.00, regardless of the distance traveled, as Pace uses a flat fare system. This means passengers pay the same amount whether they ride a short or long route.
